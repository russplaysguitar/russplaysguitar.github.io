About me

I've always found writing "About me" pages to be pretty awkward, but I'll do my best. I'm a software developer by day, and an software entrepreneur by night. I want to build things that make the world a better place. My favorite language is Javascript, but I have written useful things in many other languages. I'm literally one class away from having a B.S. in Computer Information Systems from California State University, Chico.

I play a few instruments (with various levels of success): Guitar, bass, keyboard, and drums. I've been known to sing a little, too.

I ride my bike (or walk) to work every day and occasionally go on ~20 mile rides in the countryside.

I like taking artful/landscape photos, whether it is on my old DSLR, medium-format Diana, or just Instagram. I even did a bit of 35mm black/white darkroom photography a few years back.
